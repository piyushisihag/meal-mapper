Food is more than fuel.

Savora is a college micro project dedicated to exploring what makes food truly delicious — from ingredient science to plating aesthetics. We believe anyone can cook extraordinary meals with the right knowledge.

Our goal: bring culinary wisdom to everyday kitchens through clean, actionable insights.

— What We Offer

The Secret to Delicious

🔥

Heat Mastery

Understand Maillard reactions, caramelisation, and how temperature transforms raw ingredients into complex, layered flavours.

🧂

Seasoning Science

Salt doesn't just add saltiness — it suppresses bitterness and amplifies sweetness. Learn to season like a chef, not a cook.

🫒

Fat & Flavour

Fat carries aroma molecules and creates mouthfeel. Discover which fats to use, when to add them, and why it matters.

🍋

Acid Balance

A squeeze of lemon at the end brightens everything. Learn how acidity cuts richness and lifts flat-tasting dishes instantly.

🌿

Herb Timing

Robust herbs go in early; delicate ones go in last. Get the timing right and unlock layers of freshness in every bite.

🎨

Plating & Colour

We eat with our eyes first. Colour contrast, height, and negative space on a plate make food taste better before the first bite.
